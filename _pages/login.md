## About Student Access

This course website uses Firebase authentication to track student engagement and participation. By logging in, your activity on the site will be recorded for course assessment purposes.

### Login Methods

**Option 1: Email & Password**  
Use the credentials provided by your instructor.

**Option 2: GitHub**  
Sign in with your GitHub account (you must be pre-authorized by the instructor).

### What is Tracked?

- Login and logout times
- Pages visited
- Time spent on the site
- General activity patterns

This information helps ensure student engagement with course materials and is used for participation grading.

### Need Help?

If you're having trouble logging in:
1. Make sure you're using the correct email/password provided by your instructor
2. For GitHub login, ensure you've been authorized by the instructor
3. Check that JavaScript is enabled in your browser
4. Contact your instructor if problems persist

### Protected Content

Once logged in, you'll have access to:
- Assignment materials
- Additional course resources
- Student-only announcements
- Interactive course content

**Technical Note:** This site uses Firebase Authentication, which is compliant with educational data privacy standards. Your personal information is protected and only used for course-related purposes.
