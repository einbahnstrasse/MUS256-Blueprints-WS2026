Original works by students of the Bates Sound Studio for fixed media electronic composition. Experience an evening of immersive surround-sound listening in 8 speakers.

Presented by The Bates Department of Music.

The works presented tonight
are the culminating projects
by musical creators enrolled in
Soundscapes: Recording and Designing Sound
taught by Prof. Louis Goldford.

How we make sounds move around you:
colored dots represent sounds,
and blocks represent speakers.
Composing with movement!
